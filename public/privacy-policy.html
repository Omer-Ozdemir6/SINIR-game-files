Privacy Policy

PERISHED  ·  Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PERISHED ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Summary

PERISHED is a single-player, offline story-driven game. We do not run any backend server, we do not use analytics or advertising services, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or to any third party. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require account creation, sign-in, or any personal identifiers.

Information Stored on Your Device

To let you save and resume your progress, the App stores the following data locally on your device only, which never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Game progress (current chapter, story flags, in-game stats, collected notes/documents)
  • App settings (language, text speed, sound, haptics, visual effects preferences)
  • Whether you have unlocked paid content (see "Purchases" below)

This data is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App or manually clear the App's data/storage from your device settings.

Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock additional story content. All purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not receive, process, or store your payment details (card numbers, billing address, etc.) — this information is handled directly by Google. We only receive confirmation of whether a purchase was completed, which is used locally on your device to unlock content.

For details on how Google handles payment and account information, see the Google Play Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

Permissions

  • Internet access — required by the Google Play Billing library to process purchases.
  • Vibration — used for optional haptic feedback during gameplay.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit personal data.

Third-Party Services

The App relies on Google Play Services (specifically Google Play Billing) to process in-app purchases. Google's handling of any data involved in that process is governed by Google's own privacy policy, linked above. We do not integrate any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs, and no data is shared with any other third party.

Children's Privacy

PERISHED is a horror game containing intense tension, violence, and disturbing themes, and is not directed at or intended for use by children. We do not knowingly collect any information from children, consistent with the fact that we do not collect personal information from any user.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us, you can delete all App data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage/cache from your device settings. We have no servers and therefore hold no copy of your data to delete on our end.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this same location with an updated "Last updated" date.
